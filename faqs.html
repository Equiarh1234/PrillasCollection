Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact us and we'll help you right away.

How do I place an order?

Browse our collections, select the item you like, choose size/colour if applicable, then click Buy Now. When you're ready, fill the form that will appear, now click on Send Via Email, your default email will open with a pre-filled email containing your order details. Simply review the information and click send to place your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, mobile money (where available), and other online payment methods listed at checkout. All payments are processed securely.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping option selected at checkout. Standard delivery within the same city typically takes 1–3 business days; national deliveries usually take 3–7 business days. International shipping times vary — see the shipping page for full details.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Yes — we accept returns or exchanges within 7–14 days of delivery (timeframe may vary by product). Items must be unused, with original tags and packaging. Custom-made items are not eligible for returns unless there is a manufacturing defect. See our Returns & Exchanges page for the full policy and steps to start a return.

How do I choose the right size?

Each product page includes a size guide — please check it before ordering. If you need help, send us your measurements via the contact form and we'll recommend the best size.

Can I request a custom or made-to-measure item?

Yes! We offer custom fittings and bespoke designs. Visit our Custom Orders page or contact us with your requirements. Custom orders may require an alternative timeline and deposit.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties except as needed to process your order (e.g., shipping providers) or as required by law. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How can I track my order?

After your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking details. You can also log into your account and check order status anytime.

Do you offer discounts for bulk or wholesale purchases?

Yes — we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team with order details and we'll send a quote.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the contact page, email at priscyfashion@gmail.com, or by phone at the number listed on our Contact page. We aim to respond within 1 business day.

Still have questions?

Our customer service team is here to help you with any additional questions you may have.
